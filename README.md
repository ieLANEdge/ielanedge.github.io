## 🌐 About ieLAN Edge

**ieLAN Edge** is a technology and innovation company headquartered in **Noida, India**, building purposeful technology solutions for businesses across India and globally.

The name **ieLAN** (إعلان) means *"declaration"* in Arabic — and **Edge** represents operating at the frontier of what is possible. Together, they define who we are: a company that declares a new kind of technology is possible — bold, purposeful, and built to last.

Our guiding philosophy is **كُن — Kun Fayakun** (*"Be, and it is"*). When vision meets action, transformation becomes inevitable.

> *"From Amal (hope) to Impact — one purposeful step at a time."*

## 📁 Website Structure

```
ielanedge.github.io/
│
├── index.html                  # Home page
├── manifest.json               # PWA manifest
├── sw.js                       # Service Worker (offline support)
├── sitemap.xml                 # SEO sitemap
├── robots.txt                  # Search engine instructions
│
├── pages/
│   ├── about.html              # About Us
│   ├── services.html           # All Services
│   ├── solutions.html          # Solutions by segment
│   ├── ventures.html           # Our Ventures
│   ├── newsroom.html           # News & Insights
│   ├── careers.html            # Careers & Jobs
│   └── contact.html            # Contact Us
│
└── assets/
    ├── css/
    │   └── global.css          # Shared styles
    └── js/
        ├── layout.js           # Nav & footer injection
        └── main.js             # Core JS — cursor, theme, PWA
```
